About


Primax Famplus Limited is driven to provide an innovative means of power energy generation using the abundance natural resources thereby ensuring eco friendliness as well as sustainability.
We pride ourselves by providing a highly efficient and clean energy using our hybrid generator. We are your sure provider of stable power supply.


Why Us?


Our systems produce distributed, base-load, green-energy that is highly efficient, fully scalable and is completely free of carbon emissions. It can supply stand-alone/off-grid production, as well as seamless integration to any grid. Its custom configurations may be sized anywhere from 60 kW to 50 MW and are globally compatible.
With a minimal footprint, multiple 50 MW installations can be strategically situated in population dense areas to maximize grid efficiency and stability. Each 60kW unit can power about 45 homes in the US.
